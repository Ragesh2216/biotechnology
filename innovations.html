PILLAR 02

CRISPR Therapeutic Engineering

Our CRISPR technology division develops base editing and prime editing tools for monogenic disease correction, CAR-T cell engineering, and ex vivo haematopoietic stem cell therapy programs.

We have achieved 97.3% on-target efficiency in primary T-cells using our optimised electroporation + LNP hybrid delivery system — a world-class benchmark for solid-tumour CAR-T applications.

PILLAR 03

Nanotechnology Biosensors

Stackly's nanotechnology lab synthesises graphene-based field-effect transistors and plasmonic nanorods as ultrasensitive biosensing surfaces. We've demonstrated single-molecule detection of troponin-I and miR-21 without amplification — enabling Stage 0 cancer and acute MI detection from a single blood drop.

Our lab-on-chip platform integrates nucleic acid extraction, amplification, and electrochemical detection in a credit-card-sized microfluidic cartridge, designed for rural point-of-care diagnostics at sub-₹200 per test.
