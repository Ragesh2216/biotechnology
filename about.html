Our Story

Built by Biotechnology Scientists, for Science

Founded in Salem, India — Stackly exists to democratise access to cutting-edge biotechnology tools and research platforms.

Mission

Democratising Biotechnology

Stackly's mission is to make advanced biotechnology accessible and affordable for every researcher, clinician, and institution — regardless of geography or funding level.

Our open-API platform integrates with any laboratory information system, enabling researchers in rural India to access the same sequencing and bioinformatics power as world-leading genome centres like the Broad Institute or Wellcome Sanger.

Milestones

Our Biotech Journey

From a university spin-out to South India's leading biotechnology platform.

2019
Stackly Biotech Founded
Spun out of IIT Madras with DST NIDHI grant. Focused on portable molecular diagnostic devices.
2020
First PCR Diagnostic Kit Launched
Developed rapid SARS-CoV-2 RT-PCR kit deployed in 120 Tamil Nadu government hospitals.
2021
Series A — $8M for Genomics Platform
Expanded into whole-genome sequencing and CRISPR tool development. Team grew to 60 scientists.
2022
South Asian Variant Database Launch
Published the region's largest catalogue of South Asian genetic variants — 12M+ SNPs across 15,000 individuals.
2024
2,500 Genomes Sequenced Milestone
Stackly's genomics laboratory reaches 2,500 whole-genome sequences with industry-leading 99.9% accuracy.
